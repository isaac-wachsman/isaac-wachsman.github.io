Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Isaac Wachsman. I am a computer science and mathematics student at the University of Florida. I have been tutoring now primarily in mathematics for just over 3 years. I have always loved mathematics and teaching, so my goal is to provide quality instruction at a fair rate and on a flexible schedule.

My philosophy in teaching is that learning how to think critically and understand is much more important than memorization. Because of this, I try to introduce more advanced mathematical techniques and ways of thinking that are accessible to those who are not necessarily pursuing a degree in math. I believe that mathematical thinking is a skill that is applicable to virtually every field, so why not learn?

I offer tutoring sessions both virtually through Zoom and in-person at the University of Florida campus in Gainesville, FL. In addition to this, I run a YouTube channel where I go over problems. These videos are always free to watch!
